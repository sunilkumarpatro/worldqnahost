WorldQna.com Privacy Policy

Welcome to WorldQna.com. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle user data on our Q&A platform.

Information We Collect

WorldQna.com only collects and stores the questions, answers, and comments posted by users. We do not collect any personally identifiable information such as names, email addresses, or other personal details.

How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely for the purpose of facilitating the Q&A platform. We do not use this information for marketing or any other purposes.

Cookies and Tracking

WorldQna.com does not use cookies or tracking technologies to collect personal information.

Security

We take reasonable measures to ensure the security of the information collected. However, since no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Third-Party Links

WorldQna.com may contain links to third-party websites. We have no control over the content and practices of these sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. Please review this page periodically for the latest information.
